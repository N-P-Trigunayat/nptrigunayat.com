AromaCraft Candle Studio: Catalog + Custom Order Builder

Overview

AromaCraft handcrafts candles but struggled with messy, repetitive ordering processes. Customers constantly asked for scent options, jar types, and pricing — forcing the owner to explain the same details countless times. They wanted a way to make ordering beautiful, simple, and efficient.

The Problem

Custom orders were confusing for customers and stressful for the owner. Options weren't clearly displayed, price calculations varied, and order details were scattered across chats. Bulk orders had no structure either.

What I Built

I created a calming, aesthetic ordering experience:

  • A clean catalog with scents, jars, colors, and sizes
  • A custom candle builder that walks customers through choices
  • Live price calculation based on their selection
  • WhatsApp order summary that's perfectly structured
  • An admin panel listing every custom order received

How It Works

Customers design their candle step-by-step. Once done, they send a WhatsApp message that lists every chosen detail. The owner receives formatted orders that are easy to fulfil. Bulk orders finally come in clean, trackable format.

Impact

AromaCraft instantly looked more premium. Customers stopped asking repetitive questions, ordering became enjoyable, and the owner gained clarity in production planning. Custom orders increased because the experience felt polished.

Key Outcomes

  • Higher custom-order conversion
  • Zero confusion about options
  • Faster responses
  • Better brand perception
