PawHaven Pet Grooming: Slot Booking + Grooming Packages

Overview

PawHaven wanted to deliver a comfortable, organized grooming experience for pets and their owners. But with all appointments coming through random WhatsApp messages, their schedule was constantly colliding. They needed a way to display services clearly and manage slots without chaos.

The Problem

Two clients often arrived at the same time. Owners had to repeatedly type out package details. Pet information like breed or special notes got lost. And previous grooming sessions were impossible to track.

What I Built

I crafted a warm, pet-friendly booking system that offered clarity for both sides:

  • Detailed grooming packages with pricing and duration
  • A slot-based booking system to avoid overlaps
  • Pet profiles (name, breed, age, notes)
  • A grooming history log
  • WhatsApp confirmation with a pre-filled message

How It Works

Customers explore packages, choose a time, enter pet details, and confirm via WhatsApp. The groomer receives a structured message and prepares accordingly. Each completed session gets saved to the pet's history.

Impact

The grooming center began running on fixed, predictable slots. Clients understood services better, communication became faster, and regular customers appreciated seeing their pet's past sessions. The entire experience became smoother and more professional.

Key Outcomes

  • No more overlapping appointments
  • Clear service information
  • Organized pet records
  • Stronger customer satisfaction
